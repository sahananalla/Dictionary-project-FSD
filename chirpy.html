WORD Chirpy

Meaning: Cheerful, lively, and full of happiness. Describes someone in a good and energetic mood.

  • She was chirpy even on a Monday morning.
  • His chirpy voice cheered up the whole class.
  • The bird was chirpy after the rain stopped.
  • I love talking to my chirpy neighbor.
