WORD Dawdle

Meaning: Dawdle means to waste time or move slowly, especially when you should be doing something more quickly

  • Don't dawdle on your way to class—you’ll be late!
  • She dawdled over her breakfast, not wanting to go to work.
  • The children dawdled in the park, ignoring their parents' calls.
