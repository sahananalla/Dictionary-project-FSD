WORD Bibble

Meaning: To eat or drink noisily, usually in a playful or messy way. Often used for children or animals.

  • The puppy bibbled water from the bowl.
  • My little brother bibbled soup all over the table.
  • She giggled as she bibbled her milkshake.
  • The toddler bibbled loudly, enjoying his cereal.
Word Synonyms Antonyms
Bibble slurp, munch, gobble sip, nibble, fast (not eat)
