WORD Nab

Meaning: To quickly catch or grab something or someone, often suddenly or unexpectedly.

  • The police managed to nab the thief.
  • I tried to nab the last piece of cake before anyone saw.
  • She nabbed her bag and ran to the bus stop.
  • He was nabbed red-handed taking the cookies.
Word Synonyns Antonym
Nab grab, seize, snatch, catch release, drop, lose
